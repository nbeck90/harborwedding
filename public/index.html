N + I

Nick and Isabella met in 2013 for dinner one night. They talked for a little bit, and the conversation started to lull. The silence was broken by the waiter bringing them a round of drinks purchased by some unknown table in the restaurant. 3 hours later, and having to be told to leave the restaurant after getting to know each other all night, two best friends had found each other. 4 years later, they decided they wanted to spend the rest of their lives together, and Nick asked Isabella to be his wife. Fast-forward to the present where a date and location have been set, and now all they are missing is you!
